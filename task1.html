ECE EVENTS

INNOVATE

This is an event consisting of two rounds, one is circuit debugging and the other is bot controlling in which one of the two partners of the team have to control the bot provided by us to the finish line in minimum time.The bot will be both bluetooth and dpdt controlled.

PASS THE BATON

This is a type of robo race only which will need two partners in a team. First player will take the bot to a given place and then the other partner will take another bot present there to its final destination.There would also be negative marking for damaging the arenas. The result will be given on the basis of minimum time taken to complete the full journey.

ROBO WAR

It is an event for those who like the thrill..Teams will have to design remote controlled bots which can fight one on one tournament.That bot is victorious which will immobilize the other bot or throws its opponent out of the arena. Judging is based on damage,control,design of bot and strategy used.

TECHNOMAZE

Technomaze is three round event which requires the participants to use their simple, yet intriguing circuit solving capabilities. The teams compete in the Virtual Tresure hunt from that five teams will go to the next round if they successfully redraw a given circuit into paper. The final round requires to do circuit designing on breadboard. The team which completes all the rounds in minimum time wins.

CREATIVE CIRCUIT

Industries need economical circuits for any required purpose. Most industries look at how cheap and small the products are when they are deciding their final equipments. Creative circuit is a game which will provide an idea of how challenging it can be for employers working in this field to chose and appropriate element for a better purpose.

Creative circuit game is fairly simple. Simplicity of the circuit depends on the fast calculations and reasoning abilities of the participants. The game will consist of multiple teams competing with each other at the same time. Each team will have two members. All the teams will be provided with same components. The components given will vary in their rate, availability, size and demand. A presentation slide will show the list the components and their price, availability, size and demand.

The groups have to make an effective, creative, working circuit within thirty minutes from the start time. They will be provided with papers and pens to do any rough sketch or calculation. Also, breadboards will be provided to them. The teams have to make the circuit on the breadboard within the time limit. After the time is up, the inspection team will go around asking the purpose and noting down the summation of points based on-
-> 6 Rate
-> 2 Availability
-> 3 Size
-> 4.Demand.
More availability, less rate, small size and less demand of the components will be the criterion for deciding the wining team.

PACKMAN

Play live packman to win exciting prizes using robots provided by the team. The roots are capable of navigating and collecting the gold coins. There will be restrictions in the pathway, but the player should be capable of handling the robot and reach the centre of the maze to collect the golden egg in the minimum time to win. This easy game uses gaming and judging skills to fetch you an awesome prize.

MECHANICAL EVENTS

UDAN

Have you ever been fascinated to see the planes flying too high in the sky. This ISTE EVENT spread your wings and fly high with your own Rubber Powered Glider. Review your concepts of Basic Aerodynamics and Aeromodelling and win exciting prizes.

PROBLEM STATEMENT :

The participants have to build their own design of rubber powered glider having rotating propeller on any designing softwere first which must satisfy the given criteria, and then accordind to that design they will make their own model. No launcher will be provided for launching the glider.

DIMENSIONS :

-> Front wing size not more then 50cm.
-> length not more then 70cm.

RULES :

  • A team may consist of a maximum of three members.
  • If the glider will get broken in the air or before launch then the team will be disqualified.
  • Every part of the glider must be self-made , use of models having pre-fabricated parts is not allowed. You are allowed to use readymade propellers if needed.
  • You need to explain special features of design , material used in your model clearly as there will be points for innovative design.
  • The ISTE team have the right to disqualify any team in case of misbehavior.
  • Only 10 teams with their selected designs on softwere will go to the final round.

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA :

  • The judgement and point distribution for this event will be based on :
  • Innovation
  • Time of flight
  • Range of flight

2.)MANGALYAN

Ever dreamed to step on mars surface.some have already stepped on the mars not by sending humans but by sending MARS-ROVER. You have been given a opportunity to make something which can move over the martian surface. So, here is our event “MANGALYAAN” in which you need to make a vehicle similar to mars rover.

EVENT STRUCTURE:-

  • To design a vehicle which can pass the track with lot of ups and downs, many inclinations and a number of other hurdles in shortest possible time.
  • The vehicle should be based on rocker bogie mechanism.

RULES:-

  • At most three members can make a team.
  • The vehicle should not be bought. It should be self-made and should not exceed the given dimensions.
  • The dimension of vehicle should not exceed 50cm X 30cm X 30cm (lxbxh).
  • The participant should be able to explain the way and principle used in construction in proper way, otherwise their prototype will be disqualified.
  • There should be maximum 8 motors used in vehicle. If a team use more than 8 motors, they will be disqualified.
  • The suspension system should not be used in vehicle.
  • No harm should be done to arena.
  • Sharp edges must be avoided.
  • The rover should be remote control or wire control.

POINT DISTRIBUTION:- 

  • • There will be time penalty for each foul. Foul includes touching the vehicle and damaging the arena or any other unwanted activities.
  • • Time bonus will be given for innovative and better design.

JUDGING CRITERIA:-

• The team which will take least time to complete the given task after considering time bonus or time penalty points will be winner.

3.)AMPHION

You probably would have dreamed of a vehicle which can move on both land and water. Well, now you have the chance to fabricate one.

EVENT STRUCTURE

  • Build a vehicle of dimensions not more than 40cm X 30cm X 20cm (lxbxh) which can traverse on both land and water.
  • Tyre diameter should not exceed 10cm.
  • Motor used should not exceed 100.
  • Vehicle connections can be made wired or wireless for controlling purposes.
  • Design of bot should be such that it can move properly on inclinations, hurdles, and sharp turns on land and manoeuvre in water as well.

RULES AND JUDGING CRITERIA:-

  • The participants should be able to explain the construction and working of their bot.
  • Atmost 4 members can be there in a team.
  • Vehicle should be self-made and should not exceed the given dimensions.
  • It is a time constraint event with several bonuses and penalties.
  • Extra points will be awarded for innovative design.

NOTE:

Any kind of change can take place at the time of event and rules may change accordingly.

CHEMICAL EVENTS

1.)ENGINEER OF THE YEAR

OBJECTIVE

To judge and enhance the technical and analytical skills of participants through various events.

MATERIAL REQUIRED:

Each and every required material will be provided on the spot and participants have to solve the problem with the given quantity of material in the best way.

DESCRIPTION

As not every man is meant to be engineer, so engineer of the year is a fun cum technical event. This event will judge the creative and innovative skills. The participating teams have to design or tackle the problem using given set of material.

EVENT:

The events are to judge the technical and analytical skills of the participants.

RULES:

  • A team can have 3 participants at max.
  • The given problem is to be tackled in a certain time limit.
  • The problem is to tackled with the given set of material.
  • The decision of the organizing team will be considered final in case of any dispute.

2.)HIT ME IF YOU CAN

OBJECTIVE

To design a water rocket accurate enough to hit distant targets.

MATERIAL REQUIRED:

The participating team has to just design the rocket (2 liter bottle) in the best possible way.

DESCRIPTION

An event that focuses on the way the rocket has been designed and also on the accuracy and range of the rocket which works on the principles of pressure and hydraulics.

Event:

  • The participants are expected to come in teams of 3-4 each.
  • There will be a shooting sector comprising of 3 stages at fixed distance as prescribed by the organizing team.

RULES

  • A total of 3 members per team.
  • Individuals can also participate.
  • The rocket must meet the dimensions as prescribed by the Organizing team at the time of the registration.
  • The decision of the organizing team will be considered final in case of any dispute.

JUDGING CRITERIA

The rocket that clears the maximum stages and hits the maximum targets will be declared the winner along with considering the complexity of the design and aesthetics also.

3.)LAMINAIRE

OBJECTIVE

To design conduct with nozzle that converts turbulent flow to laminar flow.

DESCRIPTION

An event that focuses on the way how efficiently you turn the turbulent flow to laminar with minimum distortion using simple principles of fluid mechanics and pressure.

Event:

  • The conduct with nozzle should be designed in such a way that it projects water to the longest distance.
  • A constant supply of water to the device’s inlet will be provided.

RULES

  • A total of 2-3 members per team.
  • The nozzle at the inlet must meet the dimensions of regular household pipe for proper fit.
  • The decision of the organising team will be considered as final in any case of dispute.

JUDGING CRITERIA

The device which makes the best laminar stream for longest distance will be declared winner. Besides the distance traversed, the design of the conduit and aesthetics will also be taken into consideration.
